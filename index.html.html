The Artistry

A Mirror-Like Polish, Layer by Careful Layer

Working with resin requires precision, patience, and a deep understanding of chemical cure times. Each of our creations is poured in multiple layers over several days to create the visual "floating" depth that characterizes high-end resin art.

We use only premium, non-toxic, and UV-stabilized resin to ensure your artwork remains crystal clear and vibrant for years, without yellowing or fading.

About the Studio

Crafty Kaur

Based in the historic city of Kurukshetra, Haryana, India, Crafty Kaur is an artistic studio run with a passion for transforming memories, dried flowers, and liquid glass resin into functional luxury keepsakes.

What started as an exploration of colors and materials has blossomed into a full custom-pour design studio. From bespoke photo keepsakes and wedding flower garlands preserved in optical-grade blocks, to crystal geodes that energize your walls, our goal is to deliver breathtaking luxury that preserves your most cherished memories.
